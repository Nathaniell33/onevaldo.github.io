What is UNAI?

UNAI is a higher Education institution managed by Seventh-day Adventist Church of the western Union in Indonesia.The history of UNAI is that is was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding school der Advent zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of th evangelists of the Seventh-Day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

2.Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
